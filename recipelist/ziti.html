Ziti

Description

Ziti is a family favorite! This is an easy way to prepare a delicious meal that is quick on a weeknight!

Ingredients

  • Ziti
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water to a rapid boil. Add salt.
  2. Add ziti to the boiling water and let boil for 11 minutes.
  3. Strain the ziti and add back to pot.
  4. Mix in butter and stir pasta until melted.
  5. Add sauce and enjoy!
