Panko Chicken

Description

Chicken is a staple dinner, but is even better when made with panko breadcrumbs! This tasty dish is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Salt
  • All purpose flour
  • 3 eggs
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Butter
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the breasts in half ans season with salt.
  2. Coat the breasts in flour.
  3. Submerge the breasts in beaten egg.
  4. Cover the egg-coated breasts with panko breadcrumbs.
  5. Add butter and olive oil to cast-iron pan and let heat slightly.
  6. Fry the breasts, checking the temperature until they reach 165 degrees.
