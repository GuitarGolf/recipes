Stovetop Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is the perfect comfort food that is great on its own or as a side. This mac and cheese is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup Evaporated milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack

Steps

  1. Add the evaporated milk and water to medium pan on high heat until boiling.
  2. Add the elbow macaroni to boiling water and set time to 8 minutes.
  3. Strain the elbows and then put them back in the cooking pot.
  4. Mix in all cheeses and stir until combined.
  5. Place the mac and cheese into sprayed dish and put in oven (450 degrees) for 10 minutes.
